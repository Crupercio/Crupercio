Brainworks Gaming

Personal gaming interest

My passion lies in creating tactical video games that challenge players' strategic thinking and decision-making. I aim to establish my own game development studio called Brainworks Gaming, where I will develop a tactical game inspired by the strategic depth of Pokémon card games, the combo system of Naruto Online, and the class system and grid mechanics of Dofus.

Game Design Concepts

Hero Limit
Inspired by the Pokémon card game, my game will feature a hero limit of 6. Each hero will represent a unique chess piece movement, adding a strategic layer to team composition.
Combo System
Drawing from Naruto Online's combo system, attacks from one hero can trigger subsequent attacks from other heroes, creating a chain of strategic moves.
Elemental and Grid System
From Dofus, I will integrate an element system (air, earth, fire, water) along with grid-based movement, an action point system, and a detailed stats system to provide depth and tactical variety in gameplay.
